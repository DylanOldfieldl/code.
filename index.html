The Basics of Chess

Chess is a board game played between white and black, the goal for both sides is to checkmate their opponents king by attacking it and also blocking its escape squares.The board is 8x8 and has 32 pieces 16 a side. The pieces are arranged with the valuable pieces on the last rank and the pawns 1 square in front. The pawns can move 1 or 2 squares for their first move and 1 square every move after that, they capture 1 square diagonally.The king and queen are in the centre behind the pawns the queen being the most powerful piece which can move diagonally and in straight lines and the king being able to move 1 square in any direction and us the most important piece which needs to be protected.
